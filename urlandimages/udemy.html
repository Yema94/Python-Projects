The Local Committees

A national network of drop-in centres advises and supports students at German universities and higher education institutions seeking to organise a work placement abroad. These centres also support the international IAESTE interns completing placements at German companies and research or university institutes. The German IAESTE network includes student-run local committees and support centres located at the International Offices at the higher education institutions.
